2021
I am a PhD Candidate with Monash University, supervised by Prof. Jianfei Cai and A/Prof. Tat-Jen Cham. My PhD research topic is mainly related to inferring and manipulating 3D scenes by simply leveraging 2D data. I am more than fascinated by how fast the 3D computer vision community has developed in recent years, and it is a pleasure to work in such an interesting area.
2019 - 2021
I was a Research Assistant at IMI NTU, supervised by Prof. Jianfei Cai and A/Prof. Tat-Jen Cham. This is where I started building up basic skills for conducting research. At that time my research topic centered around enhancing emotion recognition with human prior knowledge.

I had my ups and downs in Singapore. Work got rejected thousand times, nightmare of Australia visa, covid-19 circuit breaker, friendly Chinese Malaysian flatmates, cycled around The Red Dot...
2018 - 2019
I was a Research Intern at Lenovo Research (Beijing) working with Jianfeng Wang on facial expression recognition applications.
2016 - 2018
MEng at iSEE SYSU, supervised by A/Prof. Xiaohua Xie. Those two years passed by very quickly and I mainly explored somethings related to motion deblur with deep learning.
2012 - 2016
BEng at Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU). This is the place where I know what is ''coding'' for the first time, and I managed to get a degree in Software Engineering from it in the end. I also spent a wonderful semester as an exchange student at National Chi Nan University in 2014.
